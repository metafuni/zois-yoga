Teaching

Private and group Yoga classes

Private Yoga Classes in Berlin


In my private, one on one, yoga classes I focus on your alignment, breathing, flexibility, strength and meditation.
The three areas that I specialise in are Vinyasa, Nidra and Yin Yoga with deep mediation sessions.

With several years of teaching experiences around Europe, South America, Asia and Australia I now teach and organise classes in the Berlin area.
Your gain with my classes is in your flexibility, strength, alignment in yoga positions and mediation skills for breath, body and mind.

Group Yoga Lessons

Professional Studio Lessons

My group yoga classes are varied and suitable for both beginners and advanced. I teach on a weekly base at the Hi!Yoga studio in Berlin on Saturdays and occasional one-off advanced group sessions. I focus on strong, but conscious yoga: building up from breath work or meditation, through a warm- up, into a powerful flow with strong holds and attention to alignment.

Scheduling and prices for group classes are available on request.

Advanced (Hatha) Vinyasa

A more advanced class, in which we blend strength, balance, and flexibility.

The class gives room for more advanced poses, inversions and deep stretches, while providing modifications for less experienced yogi's who would still like to challenge themselves.

While providing a challenge, we will also work on improving your basis with micro-adjustments to improve alignment in all poses.
