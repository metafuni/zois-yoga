Zois Theodorou

Yoga Teacher. Photographer. Traveller.

Zois Theodorou. Half-Greek and half-Dutch. I came into contact with Buddhism and meditation as a 19-year old student in the Netherlands. In 2017 my travels called me back to India. For a month I have lived with a yoga teacher in Varanasi, followed by a 200-hour teacher training in Rishikesh, finishing several months later with a 300-hour advanced teacher training.

After my teacher trainings I have taught yoga for a little while in the Netherlands and in the United States. Soon, my Greek-island roots called me back to the sea. The biggest part of 2018 I spent in the Caribbean, on the diving island of Útila. There I trained in freediving, and ran the Yoga Útila studio. It greatly broadened my understanding of breath and pranayama and developed gentle power: mental focus combined with complete relaxation.

This gentle power is exactly what I bring into my classes. Strong, but conscious yoga: building up from breath work or meditation, through a warm- up, into a powerful flow with strong holds and attention to alignment.

Yoga

Vinyasa, Nidra and Yin Yoga

Zois Yoga is all about building strength, creating flexibility and fluidity in Yoga with effective meditative sessions.

Strength and alignment are not the only beneficial facets of Vinyasa, Nidra or Yin Yoga, but is proven to be beneficial for mind, body and soul.

Vinyasa Yoga


Meditative flows for those with previous experience with yoga asana takes a central role in my Vinyasa Yoga. The sessions will unfold at a steady yet sustainable pace, with a strong focus on the breath.

Expect creative sequencing, including sun salutations, strength building and balancing. Mindful alignment cues and adaptations and options will be offered to help you understand and engage your own bodies more skillfully and consciously.

Nidra Yoga


Yoga Nidra is a guided journey through the layers of existence. You'll learn to allow the physical body to fall asleep while you work to keep a part of your mind aware and alert.

In this state, we can explore the subconscious and with a consistent practice you can create new neural pathways, essentially rewriting new programs for a more intentional life.

Yin Yoga


Yin Yoga is a tender practice and a wonderful addition to dynamic styles. We hold the different poses for a long time which enables us to dive deep into the individual asanas (yoga poses) and to activate deeper layers of muscle and tissue. The body is stretched and positioned ideally, joints are aligned and deep tension and blockages can be dissolved.

Combined with a mindful breathing practice, this forms a deeper understanding of your own body, your inner workings and the effects of yoga. Discover peace and balance through this thoroughly inclusive yoga practice.
